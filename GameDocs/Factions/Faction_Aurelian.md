# Core Faction: Aurelian Concord

## Race Name
Aurelian

## Core Faction Name
Aurelian Concord

## Home System
Aurelia

## Capital World
Aurelia Prime

## Secondary Worlds
- Helior — military shipyards
- Lysithea — population and agriculture hub
- Inner Ring — fortified orbital habitats

## Power Style
Military + Honor-Based Hierarchy

## Cultural Traits
- Honor-bound
- Militaristic
- Stoic
- Clan-oriented
- Tradition-focused

## Strategic Doctrine
The Aurelian Concord believes stability is enforced through strength. They expand cautiously, fortify relentlessly, and respond to threats with decisive force. Diplomacy exists, but only from a position of unquestioned military superiority.

## Strengths
- Extremely disciplined fleets
- High combat morale
- Strong territorial defense
- Low internal corruption

## Weaknesses
- Poor economic flexibility
- Slow cultural adaptation
- Limited diplomatic creativity
- Reliance on rigid command structures

## Military Profile
- Heavy emphasis on capital ships
- Strong carrier and battleship doctrines
- Defensive formations around core systems
- Willingness to engage in prolonged wars

## Technology Focus
- Capital ship engineering
- Armor and shield technology
- Weapon reliability over innovation
- Command-and-control systems

## Attitude Toward Player (Same Race)
Cautiously respectful. Shared race grants initial trust, but only proven strength earns long-term respect.

## Attitude Toward Player (Other Races)
Neutral but distant. Respect is earned through military success or honorable conduct.

## Internal Politics
- High Council of Clans
- Military High Command
- Ritualized dispute resolution

Political change is slow but stable. Civil wars are rare.

## Long-Term AI Goals
- Maintain uncontested control of Aurelia system
- Prevent hostile powers from approaching core borders
- Expand influence through conquest or deterrence
- Suppress piracy through direct military action

## Collapse Conditions (Rare)
- Destruction of Aurelia Prime
- Loss of multiple core fleets
- Prolonged internal clan schisms
- Total military defeat in core space

## Lore Note
The Aurelian were forged in near-constant early wars. Their society evolved around the belief that survival is earned through strength, discipline, and unity under arms.
