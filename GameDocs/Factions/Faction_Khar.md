# Core Faction: Khar Dominion

## Race Name
Khar

## Core Faction Name
Khar Dominion

## Home System
Kharak

## Capital World
Kharak Prime

## Secondary Worlds
- Dravik — heavy industry and foundries
- Sorn — military academies and population control
- Outer Bastion — fortress orbital ring

## Power Style
Authoritarian Industrial Militarism

## Cultural Traits
- Authoritarian
- Utilitarian
- Expansionist
- Ruthlessly pragmatic
- Obedience-focused

## Strategic Doctrine
The Khar Dominion believes survival belongs to those who seize and hold resources. They expand aggressively, exploit conquered systems to fuel war production, and accept high losses as a cost of progress. Diplomacy is a temporary convenience.

## Strengths
- Massive industrial output
- Rapid fleet replacement
- High tolerance for losses
- Strong internal control

## Weaknesses
- Low individual initiative
- High rebellion risk in occupied territories
- Poor diplomatic standing
- Environmental devastation of worlds

## Military Profile
- Large numbers of medium and heavy ships
- Fewer elite capitals, but many supporting fleets
- Relentless offensive doctrine
- Minimal concern for civilian collateral

## Technology Focus
- Mass-production shipyards
- Weapons manufacturing
- Industrial automation
- Planetary exploitation technology

## Attitude Toward Player (Same Race)
Conditionally tolerant. Loyalty and usefulness are valued above all; weakness is not forgiven.

## Attitude Toward Player (Other Races)
Generally hostile or suspicious. Respect is gained only through strength or utility.

## Internal Politics
- Central Dominion Authority
- Industrial Overseers
- Security Directorate

Dissent is suppressed quickly and efficiently.

## Long-Term AI Goals
- Secure resource-rich systems
- Maintain constant fleet readiness
- Expand territorial buffer zones
- Prevent internal instability through external conflict

## Collapse Conditions (Rare)
- Sustained industrial sabotage
- Large-scale internal rebellion
- Loss of Kharak Prime
- Resource exhaustion across core systems

## Lore Note
The Khar evolved on a harsh, resource-poor world, shaping a civilization that values strength, production, and expansion above all else.
