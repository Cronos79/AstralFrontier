# Core Faction: Noctyr Veiled Directorate

## Race Name
Noctyr

## Core Faction Name
Veiled Directorate of Noctyr

## Home System
Umbra

## Capital World
Umbra Prime

## Secondary Worlds
- Whisper — intelligence academies and covert training habitats
- Black Archive — deep-space data vaults
- Null Ring — stealth research and black-ops staging

## Power Style
Intelligence + Subversion + Shadow Control

## Cultural Traits
- Secretive
- Patient
- Manipulative
- Risk-averse in public, ruthless in private
- Information-centric

## Strategic Doctrine
The Noctyr believe knowledge is the only permanent form of power. They expand influence through espionage, misinformation, sabotage, and proxy manipulation. Open war is avoided unless secrecy has already failed.

## Strengths
- Exceptional intelligence networks
- Superior counterintelligence
- High success in covert operations
- Ability to destabilize stronger factions indirectly

## Weaknesses
- Limited overt military strength
- Reliance on secrecy
- Severe consequences if exposed
- Distrusted by all factions

## Military Profile
- Small, elite fleets
- Stealth-capable vessels
- Precision strikes and assassinations
- Avoids prolonged engagements

## Technology Focus
- Stealth systems
- Surveillance and signal interception
- Cyberwarfare and misinformation tech
- Covert logistics and cloaking

## Attitude Toward Player (Same Race)
Cautiously permissive. The Directorate observes before trusting; usefulness is remembered.

## Attitude Toward Player (Other Races)
Formally neutral, secretly intrusive. Player actions are monitored continuously.

## Internal Politics
- Directorate Council
- Shadow Cells
- Information Custodians

Power struggles are silent and often invisible.

## Long-Term AI Goals
- Maintain informational superiority over all factions
- Prevent any single power from achieving total dominance
- Shape conflicts from the shadows
- Preserve Umbra system secrecy

## Collapse Conditions (Rare)
- Exposure of core intelligence networks
- Loss of Umbra Prime
- Successful purge by rival intelligence forces
- Internal data schism

## Lore Note
The Noctyr learned early that empires fall, fleets burn, and markets collapse—but secrets endure.
