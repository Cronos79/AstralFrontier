# Core Faction: Humans

## Race Name
Humans

## Core Faction Name
United Terran Directorate (placeholder)

> _Note: Faction name can be changed later without affecting race identity._

## Home System
**Sol**

## Capital World
**Earth**

## Secondary Worlds
- Mars — industrial / military production
- Luna — administrative and financial hub
- Sol Belt — resource backbone and shipyard infrastructure

## Power Style
**Political + Economic**

Humans rule through layered bureaucracy, treaties, trade leverage, and economic pressure rather than raw military dominance alone.

## Cultural Traits
- Pragmatic
- Bureaucratic
- Adaptable
- Politically fragmented
- Long institutional memory

## Strategic Doctrine
- Maintain control of key gate chokepoints
- Use economic sanctions before open war
- Prefer proxy conflicts and influence campaigns
- Defend core territory aggressively
- Avoid total war unless existentially threatened

## Strengths
- Strong economy and trade networks
- High population and industrial output
- Flexible diplomacy
- Resilient logistics and supply chains

## Weaknesses
- Slow decision-making
- Internal political division
- Overextended trade routes
- Vulnerable frontier holdings

## Military Profile
- Balanced fleets
- Fewer but highly capable capital ships
- Heavy reliance on logistics and fleet coordination
- Strong system and gate defenses near core space

## Technology Focus
- Gate infrastructure
- Logistics optimization
- Fleet coordination and command systems
- Defensive station technology

## Attitude Toward Player (Human)
- Starts slightly positive
- Reduced tariffs and easier docking
- No territorial tolerance inside core systems
- Rapid escalation if the player threatens Human core interests

## Attitude Toward Player (Non-Human)
- Neutral by default
- Heavily reputation-based
- Pragmatic and transactional

## Internal Politics
- Corporate blocs
- Planetary governments
- Military command councils
- Trade guilds

Internal conflicts are common but rarely destabilize the core unless under extreme pressure.

## Long-Term AI Goals
- Preserve Sol and inner systems at all costs
- Maintain economic dominance
- Prevent rival factions from controlling major trade corridors
- Suppress piracy near core space
- Contain rising player factions diplomatically first, militarily second

## Collapse Conditions (Rare)
- Loss of Sol
- Economic collapse from sustained trade disruption
- Prolonged multi-front wars
- Internal political fragmentation

## Lore Note
Humans were among the first interstellar civilizations to formalize large-scale gate networks, shaping much of the current galactic trade topology.
